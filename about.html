About

BTS, short for Bangtan Sonyeondan, is a seven member Korean Pop Boy band which debuted on 13th June, 2013 with their album '2 Cool 4 Skool' and 'No More Dream' as the title song. Since then, the band has released several albums and has held numerous concerts all around the world.

The septet consists of RM (Leader), Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V and Jungkook and most of them are credited with composition and production of many of their own songs, unlike the culture of factory produced music in South Korea.The record breaking group has been honoured with many prestigious awards as well as nominations in the music industry both in Korea and Worldwide. Their fans who they call A.R.M.Y, standing for Adorable Representation MC for Youth, have been acknowledged as the biggest fandom in the world. They have time and again proved their loyalty to the boy group by not just voting for their victory, but also by donating thousands of dollars for various good

Their label HYBE Labels (then BigHit Entertainment) is home to many record breaking artists worldwide like Ariana Grande, Justin Bieber, Demi Lovato, TXT and such.
